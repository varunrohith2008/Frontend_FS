Thalapathy Vijay

Vijay, fondly called “Thalapathy” by his fans, is one of the most celebrated and influential actors in Tamil cinema. Born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, he is the son of noted filmmaker S.A. Chandrasekhar. Vijay made his debut as a child artist and later rose to stardom with his charismatic screen presence, versatile acting, and exceptional dance skills. Over the years, he has delivered numerous blockbuster films that appeal to audiences across generations, blending action, comedy, romance, and social messages. Known for his strong fan base not just in Tamil Nadu but worldwide, Vijay has become a cultural icon, with his movies often creating festival-like celebrations upon release. Beyond films, he is admired for his humility, philanthropy, and connection with people, which has cemented his place as one of the leading stars of Indian cinema.

Ajith Kumar

Ajith Kumar, often referred to as “Thala” by his fans, is one of the most popular and respected actors in Tamil cinema. Born on May 1, 1971, in Hyderabad, he initially pursued a career in racing before entering the film industry. Over the years, Ajith has built a strong reputation not only for his versatile acting skills but also for his simplicity and humble nature off-screen. He has acted in a wide range of films, from romantic dramas and family entertainers to action thrillers, earning both critical acclaim and a massive fan following. Despite his stardom, Ajith is known for staying away from public events and maintaining a private lifestyle, focusing on his family and passion for racing. His dedication, discipline, and down-to-earth personality have made him not just a superstar, but also a role model for millions of fans.
